Biography

    I am a Postdoc Research Associate in DeSI lab at Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute (AAII), University of Technology Sydney (UTS), working with Dist. Prof. Jie Lu since June 2024. I received my Ph.D degree in computer science at UTS in 2024. My research interests lie in streaming data mining, concept drift, multimodal analysis and so on.

Research Interests

    My research interests lie in providing mathematical and theoretical foundations to justify and understand machine learning models and designing efficient learning algorithms for problems in the field of streaming data mining, concept drift, and multimodal analysis. with a particular emphasis on:

    • Adative learning under concept drift

    • Temporal domain generalization

    • Multimodal analysis
